About me..

My name is Boomer...I know "is that really your name?"...well, its a nickname but I've been Boomer since I was a kid. What can I say, the name has grown on me.

Speaking of childhood, I also grew up with computers playing games (Commander Keen!), tweaking with hardware (sometimes to my parents dismay but I did need to splice those wires), messing with software and building small games. Today I still love messing with computers, programming, and video games. On top of this I also love social science and generally figuring out what makes people tick. Right now I am a MScEcon candidate at Aberystwyth University working on complexity theory in social science (with my sights set on computer simulation models).

In my free time I love to work on programming projects (such as this site) and plan to start programming on my new Raspberry Pi while working on learning more about C (refreshing my high school C++ skills). Feel free to look around my site and see what projects I have going on! Welcome :)
